# Investment Framework

Garuda uses a structured scoring framework to help an investor evaluate Indian AI startups consistently.

## Scoring Scale

Each scoring dimension uses a 1-5 scale:

- 1: Weak
- 2: Below average
- 3: Acceptable or uncertain
- 4: Strong
- 5: Exceptional

Scores should include notes. The notes matter as much as the number because they preserve reasoning.

## Scoring Dimensions

- Market opportunity
- Founder quality
- Product strength
- AI defensibility
- Traction
- Distribution
- Funding signal
- Strategic fit
- Risk level

## Thesis Structure

Each company thesis should include:

- Bull case
- Bear case
- Key questions
- Market view
- Founder assessment
- Product assessment
- Distribution assessment
- AI defensibility assessment
- Risks
- Decision memo
- Next action

## Signal Taxonomy

Track signals such as:

- Hiring activity
- Product launch
- Partnership
- Funding news
- Customer win
- Media mention
- Regulatory signal
- Founder update
- Other

## Company Statuses

Suggested statuses:

- Watching
- Researching
- Shortlisted
- Passed
- Invested

## Decision Discipline

Garuda should help preserve why a company looked interesting, why it was passed, what changed, and what the investor should do next.

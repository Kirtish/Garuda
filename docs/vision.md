# Vision

Garuda is a local-first Venture Intelligence Platform focused on Indian AI startups.

The platform enables an individual investor to discover promising AI startups, track funding rounds, research founders, build investment portfolios, record investment theses, score companies using a structured framework, and monitor signals such as hiring, product launches, partnerships, and funding activity.

The long-term vision is to evolve Garuda into a professional-grade AI-powered venture research system.

## Product Belief

Venture research is strongest when scattered market signals become structured, durable, and reviewable. Garuda should help an investor move from raw information to conviction.

## Local-First Principle

Garuda should protect the investor's private research workflow. Structured records, notes, theses, and decision logs should be portable and inspectable without depending on cloud services.

## Target User

The MVP is built for an individual investor researching Indian AI startups manually or semi-manually.

## Long-Term Direction

Garuda may later support AI-assisted summarization, entity extraction, scoring support, signal monitoring, comparison views, and richer portfolio intelligence. These capabilities should build on the local-first foundation rather than replace it.
